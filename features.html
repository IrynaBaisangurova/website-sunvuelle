Why choosing us!

Few reasons why people choosing us!

1.Breed quality: The cat cattery is breeding Persian pedigree cats, which means that you can be sure of the quality of your cat. You will get a healthy and beautiful kitten that will meet the breed's standards.

2.Health and vaccinations: The cat cattery monitors the health of its animals and provides them with vaccinations. This means that your pet will be protected from various diseases and infections.

3. Support and advice: The cat cattery provides its customers with support and advice on the care of their pets. You can get advice on feeding, coat care and the health of your kitten. This will help you ensure your cat's best health and well-being.

4.Socialization: Our kittens are raised in a home environment and receive plenty of attention and care, which helps them socialize and adjust to their new home.

5.Shipping: We provide shipping services for our kittens anywhere in the world, which makes it convenient and safe for our customers to purchase an animal.
